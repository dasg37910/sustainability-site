SOC Analyst Sustainability Analysis

This project evaluates the career path of a SOC Analyst across three pillars: Economic, Social, and Environmental sustainability.


Executive Summary

As organizations increasingly rely on digital infrastructure, the demand for SOC analysts continues to grow, making it an important and relevant career path to examine.

From an economic perspective, the SOC analyst role offers strong sustainability due to high demand, competitive salaries, and long-term job stability.

Socially, SOC analysts play a critical role in protecting individuals, businesses, and communities from cyber threats such as data breaches and identity theft.

From an environmental standpoint, the career relies on always-on systems and energy-intensive data centers, but improvements in cloud efficiency are reducing impact.

Overall, the SOC analyst career path demonstrates strong economic and social sustainability with improving environmental performance.
