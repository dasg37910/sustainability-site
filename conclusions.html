Conclusions

Findings and Recommendations

  • Finding for Economic Pillar:
     The findings indicate that the SOC analyst career path is highly economically sustainable due to strong and growing demand, competitive salaries, and diverse opportunities across multiple industries. With cybersecurity roles projected to grow much faster than average and thousands of open positions globally, job stability and advancement potential are significant. Salaries increase steadily with experience, and additional benefits further enhance overall compensation. However, the field is not easily accessible at the entry level, as it requires a solid technical foundation and relevant skills, meaning individuals must invest in education and experience to become competitive candidates.
